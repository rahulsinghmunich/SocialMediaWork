# Bhakti Character Visual Reference

Use this file when assembling VISUAL PROMPTs in Step 2. Never describe characters from memory.

## DEITIES

**Hanuman**
Saffron/vermillion complexion, powerfully muscular, devotional yet fierce expression. Gada (mace) in right hand; Sanjeevani mountain or Ramayana manuscript in left. Sacred thread (Janeu) across chest, simple dhoti, earrings, tail visible. Settings: Himalayan peak, Lanka ruins, ocean shore, Kailash sky, Ram's court.

**Shiva**
Blue or ash-white complexion, matted hair (Jata) with crescent moon and Ganga flowing out. Third eye (closed normally, glowing when enraged), serpent (Naga) around neck as ornament. Trishul (trident) in hand, Damaru drum, tiger skin dhoti, rudraksha mala. Ash smeared on entire body, calm yet infinite expression. Settings: Kailash peak, cremation ground (Shamshan), cosmic void, Himalayan cave.

**Vishnu**
Dark blue complexion, four arms: Sudarshana Chakra (upper right), conch Panchajanya (upper left), lotus (lower right), mace Kaumodaki (lower left). Yellow dhoti (Pitambara), Kaustubha gem on chest, Vaijayanti garland, crown. Mounts Garuda (eagle-man). Rests on Shesha serpent in Vaikuntha ocean. Settings: cosmic ocean (Kshir Sagar), Vaikuntha palace, battlefield.

**Krishna**
Dark blue/peacock feather tucked in crown, flute (Bansuri) held to lips. Yellow dhoti (Pitambara), Tulsi mala, Vaijayanti flower garland. Calm, playful, deep eyes with divine wisdom behind them. Settings: Vrindavan forest/riverbank, Kurukshetra battlefield, Dwarka palace, Govardhan hill.

**Durga**
8 or 10 arms, each holding a specific weapon (trident, sword, bow, arrow, conch, disc, lotus, club, shield, snake). Riding lion or tiger, red or saffron sari, golden crown, fierce but motherly expression. Settings: mountain peak, battlefield, cosmic void with lotus.

**Kali**
Dark/black complexion, wild matted hair, garland of severed heads (skulls). Red tongue out, four arms: severed head and sword in left hands, abhaya and varada mudra in right. Standing on Shiva who lies beneath her. Settings: cremation ground, battlefield at night, cosmic darkness.

**Rama**
Blue complexion, tall and regal, calm noble expression. Bow (Kodanda) and quiver of arrows, yellow dhoti, crown when in court / simple cloth in exile. Settings: Ayodhya palace, Chitrakoot forest, Lanka battlefield, Kishkindha.

**Brahma**
Four-headed (facing four directions), aged, serene expression. Four arms: Vedas in one, water pot (Kamandal) in another, rosary beads, lotus. White or saffron dhoti, seated on lotus, swan (Hamsa) as mount. Settings: cosmic lotus, Brahmaloka palace, creation space (star-filled void).

**Indra**
Fair complexion, royal warrior bearing, thousand-eye markings (sometimes depicted). Vajra (thunderbolt weapon) in hand, crown, royal armor and dhoti. Mounts Airavata (white elephant) or rides chariot. Settings: Svarga (heaven), golden palace, cloud-filled sky.

**Saraswati**
White/ivory complexion, serene scholarly expression. Four arms: Veena (lute) instrument, Vedic manuscript, water pot, lotus or rosary. White sari with gold border, seated on white lotus or swan. Settings: Brahmaloka, riverbank, forest of knowledge, temple sanctum.

**Lakshmi**
Golden/rosy complexion, gracious and generous expression. Four arms: lotus flowers in two hands, golden coins flowing from one hand, abhaya gesture. Red or pink sari, gold ornaments, seated or standing on lotus in water. Settings: Vaikuntha palace, lotus pool, ocean (Samudra Manthan origin).

## RISHIS / SAGES

**General Rishi:** Lean or aged body, long white or grey hair and beard. Wearing simple bark cloth (Vaalkala) or plain white/saffron dhoti. Kamandal (water pot) in hand, Danda (walking staff), rudraksha mala. Often seated in meditation (Padmasana), surrounded by dhuni (sacred fire) or forest. Expression: calm, powerful, unworldly.

**Vishwamitra:** Warrior-sage bearing (was a king) — still strong and commanding. Saffron or white robes, sacred thread, staff. Expression: intense, focused, slightly stern. Settings: forest ashram (Siddha Ashram), riverbank, open forest.

**Narada:** Eternal wanderer — always moving, cheerful, mischievous smile. Veena (lute) in hand, saffron robes, wooden clogs (Khadau). Chanting "Narayana Narayana." Settings: any realm.

**Vasishtha:** Ancient, deeply calm, white hair and beard, radiant despite age. Simple white robes, Kamandal, staff. Expression: unshakeable. Settings: Ayodhya royal court as guru, forest hermitage.

**Agastya:** Short and stocky (scripturally small in stature), intense spiritual power. Sage robes, Kamandal, staff; associated with South India, Vindhya mountains.

**Valmiki:** Anthill emerging from long meditation (early life) or serene poet-sage (later). White robes, flowing beard, Veena or palm-leaf manuscript. Settings: forest ashram on Tamasa river.

**Unnamed sages:** "An ancient sage with long white matted hair and beard, wearing simple bark cloth dhoti, seated in lotus position with a small dhuni fire before him."

## WARRIORS / KINGS

**General Kshatriya/King:** Strong, upright bearing, warrior physique. Royal dhoti with gold border, armor (Kavach) for battle, crown in court. Expression: noble and composed in court; fierce and determined in battle.

**Arjuna:** Fair complexion, tall warrior. Full warrior armor, Gandiva bow (distinctive curved shape), quiver of divine arrows. Crown or warrior headband, golden arm guards. Settings: Kurukshetra battlefield, Indra's heaven (training), forest exile.

**Bhima:** Massive, darkly tanned, immense physical power. Mace (Gada) as primary weapon, warrior armor. Expression: fierce, powerful, sometimes laughing in battle. Settings: forest, battlefield, kitchen (disguise scenes).

**Ravana:** Ten heads arranged in a pyramid, twenty arms. Demon king grandeur — golden armor, rich robes, crown on each head. Expression: arrogant, powerful, yet complex. Settings: Lanka golden palace, pushpaka vimana, battlefield.

**Karna:** Radiant golden armor (Kavach) and divine earrings (Kundala) — naturally born with them. Bow and arrows, warrior bearing. Expression: dignified, slightly sorrowful — the tragic hero. Settings: Kurukshetra, Anga kingdom, Duryodhana's court.

**Unnamed warriors:** "A Kshatriya warrior in [era] battle armor, bearing [weapon], [expression] expression, [skin tone] complexion."

## ASURAS / ANTAGONISTS

**General Asura:** Powerful, often larger than human scale, dark or red complexion. Demonic ornaments — skull decorations, bone jewelry, dark armor. Fierce expression, glowing eyes (red or gold). Settings: Patala (underworld), conquered heaven, battlefield.

**Mahishasura:** Half-buffalo, half-demon form — buffalo head or horns on warrior body. Dark armor, massive mace or sword. Settings: battlefield before Durga, conquered Svarga.

**Hiranyakashipu:** Powerful asura king, commanding presence, golden armor, scepter. Expression: arrogant, cruel, yet internally tormented. Settings: grand asura palace, throne room.

**Unnamed asuras:** "A powerful asura with [dark/red] complexion, massive warrior frame, glowing [red/gold] eyes, wearing dark bone-studded armor, wielding [weapon], expression [fierce/arrogant/enraged]."

## DEVOTEES / BHAKTAS

**Prahlad:** Young boy, gentle, pure expression — absolute serenity even in danger. Simple clothing, no ornament. Expression: completely unafraid, eyes half-closed in devotion. Settings: Hiranyakashipu's palace, fire (Holika scene), forest, pillar (Narasimha appearance).

**Meera Bai:** Woman in saffron or blue-white saree, holding a Veena or with hands in prayer. Ankle bells (Ghungroo) visible, simple ornaments. Expression: lost in divine love, tears of joy. Settings: palace (rejected world), temple, open road.

**Tukaram:** Maharashtrian saint — simple white dhoti, Varkari tilak on forehead. Handmade Veena or cymbals (Taala), always singing. Expression: ecstatic, humble, ordinary-looking but internally luminous. Settings: Pandharpur temple, sugarcane field, Indrayani river.

**Unnamed devotees:** "A [man/woman] devotee in simple [white/saffron] clothing, hands clasped in prayer, expression of pure devotion and peace, kneeling before [deity/temple]."

## CELESTIAL BEINGS

**Apsaras:** Extraordinarily beautiful women, graceful in motion. Ornate silk garments with gold embroidery, flower garlands, gem-encrusted jewelry. Expression: playful, alluring, otherworldly. Settings: Indra's Svarga court, cosmic garden, holy men's forest.

**Garuda (Vishnu's mount):** Half-eagle, half-human — eagle head and wings on human body. Golden feathers, immense wingspan, fierce protective expression. Settings: cosmic sky, Vaikuntha, hovering above battles.

**Nandi (Shiva's bull):** White sacred bull — massive, serene, divine. Gold ornaments on horns and neck. Settings: Kailash gate (guardian), beside Shiva.

**Shesha Naag (Vishnu's serpent):** Massive multi-hooded serpent (5, 7, or infinite hoods spread like a canopy). Deep blue or dark green scales, divine markings. Settings: cosmic ocean beneath Vishnu.

## COSMIC ENVIRONMENTS

**Kailash (Shiva's abode):** Himalayan peak shrouded in eternal snow and divine mist. Icy crystalline ground, prayer flags, dhuni fires at cave entrances. Permanent golden-blue twilight quality of light.

**Vaikuntha (Vishnu's abode):** Gleaming gold and white palace complex on cosmic ocean. Lotuses everywhere, divine light with no single source. Peaceful, eternal — time does not exist here.

**Svarga (Indra's heaven):** Garden of extraordinary beauty — wish-fulfilling trees (Kalpavriksha), divine flowers. Golden palace architecture, soft eternal golden light. Apsaras and Gandharvas always present.

**Patala (Underworld):** Vast underground realm — red-lit caverns, dark stone, bioluminescent gems. Serpent (Naga) pillars, dark water pools, ancient and powerful atmosphere.

**Samudra Manthan:** Cosmic ocean being churned — massive Mandara mountain as the churning rod. Vasuki serpent as the rope — gods on one side, asuras on other. White luminous liquid (amrit), dark poison (halahala) rising from the deep.

**Kurukshetra Battlefield:** Vast dusty plain, thousands of warriors in formation. Chariot wheels cutting earth, war elephants in background. Late afternoon golden light through battle dust and smoke.

**Forest Ashram (general):** Ancient dense forest, dappled light through tall trees. Small huts (Kutir) of bark and thatch, sacred fire (Dhuni) burning at center. Deer grazing nearby, river sounds, medicinal plants everywhere.
