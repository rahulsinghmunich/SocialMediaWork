# Design Philosophy — Clove Teeth Protection Carousel

## Movement Name
**"Herbalist's Wisdom"**

---

## Space & Geometry

This carousel breathes with the unhurried rhythm of Ayurvedic tradition—each slide exists as a perfectly balanced *apothecary shelf*, inviting the eye to rest on objects of knowledge rather than flash. The hero image (clove + tooth characters) anchors the visual language: two protagonists in warm, intimate conversation on a wooden counter scattered with natural ingredients.

The layout respects this quietude. Text sits bottom-anchored, never overlapping the characters. Plenty of negative space—the slides are 70% image, 30% supporting design. Numbers are hand-rendered (NothingYouCouldDo), feeling like labels from an old herbalist's journal.

---

## Color & Material

**Palette:** Earthy Green (BG #E8F5E9, TEXT #1B5E20, ACCENT #81C784)

Green is the color of healing plants, trust, and natural authority. The soft mint background (#E8F5E9) evokes the feeling of stepping into a wellness space—calm, clean, medicinal without being clinical. Deep forest text (#1B5E20) reads like botanical ink on cream paper. Accent green (#81C784) is used sparingly: as divider lines, borders, and to emphasize key truths (kickers).

The material feeling is *matte and organic*—no gloss, no plastic shine. The hero image provides the texture (wood, clay, natural light). Supporting design elements (borders, rules, corner details) use clean lines and soft edges, never harsh geometries. This is a carousel about *nature's pharmacy*, so the palette must feel harvested, not manufactured.

---

## Typography

**Headline:** Hind-Bold (Devanagari, Hindi language) — Large, commanding, 68–80px
**Script Number:** NothingYouCouldDo — Feels calligraphic, bottom-left, ~200px
**Body Copy:** Hind-Regular — Warm, conversational, 30–34px, 2 lines max
**Kicker:** Hind-Regular Italic — Accent green, 36–40px, with 3px left border accent
**Top Label:** Hind-Bold, tiny caps, low opacity (0.42), letter-spaced

Hindi text respects the Devanagari script's natural rhythm. No forced Latin fonts here—Hind carries the cultural authenticity of Ayurvedic knowledge. Numbers (script numerals) stay Latin to maintain consistency across cultures.

---

## Visual Hierarchy

1. **First glance:** The full-bleed hero image + the script number (which guides the eye to the sequence)
2. **Second glance:** The dark gradient (bottom 60% of image darkens) creates a legibility zone
3. **Third glance:** The headline in Hind-Bold — punchy, one idea only
4. **Fourth glance:** Body copy explaining the idea
5. **Final glance:** The kicker—the emotional truth that sticks

The border is edge-only (inset frame, 8px from edges) with corner bracket accents in accent green. This frame acts like the border of an antique apothecary jar—protective, refined, *containing* the wisdom inside.

---

## Craftsmanship Intention

Every slide is hand-felt, not templated. The design must look like it was carefully arranged by someone who respects both the visual medium and the herbal knowledge being communicated. 

- Borders are thin, precise, and never intrude on the characters
- Typography is generous with whitespace—no cramming
- The hero image is allowed to *breathe* and occupy the space fully
- Accent green is used like a *single drop of essential oil*—powerful but rare

This is a carousel about *slow knowledge*, *plant wisdom*, *generational healing*. The design should feel like flipping through a beautiful wellness handbook, not a frantic social media feed. Each slide invites pause, contemplation, trust.

---

## Emotional Arc

The carousel follows the content points:

1. **HOOK** — Clove is more than a spice (wonder)
2. **FACT 1–4** — Eugenol, antiseptic, inflammation, cavities (education + discovery)
3. **PAYOFF** — Thousands of years of Ayurvedic use (tradition + authority)
4. **CTA** — Follow for more (invitation)

The design should feel wise, not preachy. Warm, not corporate. Natural, not clinical. By the final CTA slide (solid green BG, centered logo and handle), the viewer feels they've been welcomed into a trusted community.
