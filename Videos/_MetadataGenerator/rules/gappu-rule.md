# Gappu — Metadata Rule

## Channel Identity
- **Name:** Gappu Magic Story Land
- **YouTube:** Gappu Magic Story Land
- **Instagram:** @gappumagicworld
- **Facebook:** Gappu Magic Story Land
- **Niche:** Kids animated stories, 3D Pixar-style toddler monkey character
- **Target audience:** Parents of kids 3-8, kids themselves, family viewers

## Content Type
- **Format:** Shorts (9:16 vertical, under 60 sec) + Carousels
- **Character:** Gappu (3D Pixar toddler monkey with moral lessons)
- **Language:** English + Hindi mix (Hinglish for Hindi audience)

## Tone
- Playful, curious, emotional
- Child-safe always (no scary themes)
- Moral lesson embedded
- Wonder + joy + learning

## YouTube Title Formula
- Search: "Gappu and the [Object/Event] | Kids Moral Story"
- Browse: "Why Did Gappu [Emotion]? 🐒 Kids Story"
- Hybrid: "Gappu's [Adventure] - The Lesson He Learned | Moral Story for Kids"
- Keywords: gappu, kids story, moral story, animated short, pixar style kids

## Hook Writing Rules
- First 2 sec: Gappu on screen showing strong emotion
- No talking (Gappu never speaks — visual emotion only)
- Create curiosity: "What will Gappu do?"

## SEO Keywords (always include 2-3)
- gappu, kids stories, moral story, animated kids, pixar kids, bedtime story, kids animation, character animation, family viewing

## What NOT to do
- No scary content, violence, adult themes
- No music that isn't kid-friendly
- No Hindi-only (always offer English translation)
- No mature CTAs (appeal to parents, not kids directly)
