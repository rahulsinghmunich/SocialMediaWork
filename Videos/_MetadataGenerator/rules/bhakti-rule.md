# Bhakti (Sanatan Dharma) — Metadata Rule

## Channel Identity
- **Name:** Sanatan Dharma
- **YouTube:** Sanatan Dharma
- **Instagram:** @sanatandharmatheeternalpath
- **Facebook:** Sanatan Dharma
- **Niche:** Hindu devotional stories, scriptural stories, deity tales
- **Target audience:** Hindu devotees, Sanskrit/spiritual interest, age 25+

## Content Type
- **Format:** Shorts (9:16) + Carousels + Long-form devotional stories
- **Visual style:** Cinematic devotional imagery, deities
- **Language:** Hindi primary, Sanskrit for mantras, English subtitles

## Tone
- Reverent, devotional, spiritual
- Scripturally accurate
- Inspiring, uplifting
- Never casual about deities

## YouTube Title Formula
- Search: "[Deity Name] की कथा | [Scripture Name] | Sanatan Dharma"
- Browse: "भगवान [Deity] ने क्यों [Action]? | रहस्य"
- Hybrid: "[Deity]'s Untold Story from [Scripture] | Sanatan Dharma"
- Keywords: sanatan dharma, hindu story, [deity], bhakti, pauranic katha

## Hook Writing Rules
- First 2 sec: Deity visual on screen
- Sanskrit mantra or Hindi hook line
- Create reverence, not clickbait

## SEO Keywords (always include 2-3)
- sanatan dharma, bhakti, hindu mythology, [specific deity], pauranic katha, vedic, sanskrit stories, hindu religion, dharma

## What NOT to do
- No casual/irreverent tone about deities
- No Western comparisons that might offend
- No incorrect scriptural references
- No clickbait that trivializes sacred content
- Always include scriptural source if claiming a story
