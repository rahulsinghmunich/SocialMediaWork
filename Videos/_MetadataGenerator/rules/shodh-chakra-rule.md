## Hook Writing Rules (Hindi-first)
- "क्या आप जानते हैं..." (Did you know...)
- "वो रहस्य जो..." (The mystery that...)
- "भारत का छुपा हुआ..." (India's hidden...)
- Start with a surprising fact or question

## SEO Keywords (always include 2-3)
- shodh chakra, hindi documentary, indian history hindi, world history hindi, rahasya, itihas, bhugol, hindi educational

## Chapter Strategy (long videos)
- Use NotebookLM research structure → map to chapters
- Each chapter: Hindi title + English keyword
- Chapter every 1-2 minutes for 10+ min videos

## Instagram/Facebook Clip Strategy
- Extract **1-3 Reels-worthy moments** from long YouTube video
- Each clip: 30-60 sec with strongest hook
- Cliffhanger ending → "Full video link in bio"

## What NOT to do
- No sensationalism (respects research-first ethos)
- No English-only content (channel is Hindi-first)
- No unverified claims (always cite sources from NotebookLM research)
- No political/religious bias
- Don't skip Hindi (defeats the niche advantage)
