# Tattva Frequencies — Metadata Rule

## Channel Identity
- **Name:** Tattva Frequencies
- **YouTube:** Tattva Frequencies
- **Instagram:** @tattvafrequencies (confirm handle)
- **Facebook:** Tattva Frequencies (confirm page)
- **Niche:** Sanatan Dharma meditation music, bhajans, mantras, healing frequencies
- **Target audience:** Hindu spiritual seekers, meditators, yoga practitioners, 20-60

## Content Type
- **Format:** Long-form audio visualizations (5 min - 1 hour typical)
- **Production:** Suno-generated music + CapCut visual edit
- **Visuals:** Deity imagery, sacred geometry, nature, abstract frequencies

## Canonical Channel Description (use in YouTube About + post bios)

Our mission is to help you disconnect from the noise of the modern world and reconnect with your true essence (Tattva) through the power of sacred sound.

## Hook Writing Rules
- Invite the listener into a state ("Begin your morning with...")
- Name the benefit ("release stress in 10 minutes...")
- Reference the deity/mantra/frequency

## What NOT to do
- No clickbait ("BEST mantra!!!" style)
- No irreverent tone about deities
- No claims about specific healing outcomes (legal/spiritual respect)
- Don't conflate with Bhakti channel (Bhakti = story-based, Tattva = music/audio)
