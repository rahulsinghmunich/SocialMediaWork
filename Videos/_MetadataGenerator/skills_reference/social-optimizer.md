# Instagram & Facebook Optimization Rules (extracted from claude-skills)

## INSTAGRAM 2026 Algorithm Rules
- **Keywords in captions > hashtags** (IG reads captions like search engine)
- Hashtags: **3-5 max** (not 30 — old strategy is dead)
- **DM shares** = most heavily weighted signal for Reels distribution
- Alt text matters — feeds IG search
- Carousel + original audio > single image + trending audio
- First line of caption must hook in 125 chars (before "more")

## Instagram Caption Structure
- **Line 1**: Hook (curiosity / emotion / question)
- **Line 2-3**: Context (what's in the video)
- **Line 4+**: Value / story / CTA
- **Emojis**: Sparse, meaningful (2-4 max)
- **CTA**: Ask for DM share ("Send this to a friend who...")
- **Hashtags**: End of caption, 3-5 total

### Hashtag Strategy
- 1 niche hashtag (small community, <100K posts)
- 2 medium hashtags (100K-1M posts)
- 1-2 broad hashtags (1M+ posts)
- Always include branded/channel hashtag

## FACEBOOK Rules
- **First sentence** = everything (FB truncates aggressively)
- Video posts: **native upload** beats YouTube links (5-10x reach)
- Questions in captions get 2x engagement
- **Long captions okay** on FB (unlike IG) — story-driven
- Emojis: more acceptable than IG (3-5 okay)
- **Share CTAs work** on FB: "Share if you agree"

## Facebook Post Structure
- **Hook line** (with emoji okay)
- Short paragraph of context
- Story/value expansion (can be 3-4 paragraphs)
- CTA with emoji
- Hashtags at end (2-3 only — FB uses less)

## Engagement Multipliers (both platforms)
- Ask a question in caption → 2x comments
- "Save this" CTA → higher algo weight
- "Send to someone who..." → DM share signal
- Controversial/bold statement → higher reach
- Tag a friend CTA → viral coefficient

## Reels-Specific (IG + FB)
- Under 90 sec optimal
- Hook MUST be in first 1-2 sec
- Text overlay on video (not just caption)
- Trending audio if available
- End with loop-back moment (encourages replay)
- Caption tells different story than video (gives extra value)

## Carousel-Specific (IG)
- 10 slides max (use all 10 for reach boost)
- Slide 1 = hook (stops scroll)
- Slide 2-9 = value delivery
- Slide 10 = CTA + branded slide
- Mixed photo+video carousels get 1.4x reach

## Post-Level Data Signals
- **Reach rate** = impressions / followers
- **Engagement rate** = (likes + comments + shares + saves) / reach
- **Save rate** = saves / reach (most algo-weighted)
- **Share rate** = shares / reach (drives distribution)
